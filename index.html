Welcome to Crowd Business Innovation Center

Our Mission


To enhance the image of the university by producing excellent researchers and be a referral centre for ICT and business processes for crowdsourcing solutions.

Our Vision


To be a leading centre in producing researchers and experts to develop technopreneurship solutions and business solution for crowdsourcing.

Our Objective


To be a leading centre in producing researchers and experts to develop technopreneurship solutions and business solution for crowdsourcing.

About us

CBIC is a centre that aims to promote inclusive innovations, providing program supports, consultations, guidelines and becoming one stop centre for reference and other programs related to crowdsourcing. The centre is focusing on research in the area of crowdsourcing that in line with Digital Malaysia initiatives to become a high income country by 2020.
